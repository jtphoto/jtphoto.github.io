Meet Joyce Tong

Hey there, I’m Joyce! I am a DFW-based natural light photographer who specializes in portraits and weddings.

My photography style is authentic, light, and timeless. My goal is to capture and document your milestones in images that you can share and treasure for years to come.

I have been recognized for my work as a finalist in the 2018 Shoot & Share Contest, one the largest photo contests in the world, spanning 149 countries and my work has also been featured in multiple regional and national publications. I have also had the incredible opportunity to mentor and train other young photographers as they gain experience in the industry.

When I’m not working, you can catch me snacking on dark chocolate, adding new places to my travel bucket list, or hitting the trails with my rescue dog, Moose, as I train for my next race.
