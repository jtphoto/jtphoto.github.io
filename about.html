Meet Joyce Tong

Hey there, I’m Joyce! I was born and raised in Austin, Texas, and am currently studying at the University of Texas at Austin. I am a natural light photographer who specializes in senior portraits and weddings.

My photography style is authentic, light, and natural. My goal is to bring out a real laugh in you, and not just that smile you reserve for uncomfortable dinner parties and awkward encounters. My passion is for capturing the priceless candid moments in life, and preserving them forever. I want you to be able to look at the framed photo on your dresser or flip through an album years from now, and feel all of the emotions from those moments again.

As a young creative in the industry, I have been recognized for my work and placed in the top 11% of the 2016 Shoot & Share Contest, one the largest photo contests in the world, spanning 149 countries. I have also had the incredible opportunity to learn from industry pioneers like Jenna Kutcher, Jasmine Star, and Jeremy Chou, and am an assistant editor for Piper Little Post Processing, where I curate and edit images for other professional photographers.

When I’m not working, you can catch me binge-watching Game of Thrones, snacking on dark chocolate, or hitting the trails around Austin with my rescue dog, Moose, as I train for my next race.
